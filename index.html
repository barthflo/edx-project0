our tours gallery about us

Mongolia inspires our imaginations to run wild with vivid thoughts of nomadic lifestyle and exceptional landscapes. Immersing ourselves in the land of Gengis Kahn to witness the beauty of the Gobi desert.

As we strive to experience sleeping in a Ger once in our lifetime…we connect culture and wildlife together showcasing a journey with a difference. Our knowledge, expertise and love of personnel experiences drives us to know all about the Mongolia in you.

As we are ourselves from a nomadic family, our tailored Mongolian tours designed with the help of local families like ours, will bring you in an adventure you will never forget!
